Hubstaff’s Guiding Principles

Transparency is about showing users exactly how Hubstaff tracks time, activity, and apps & URLs — and ensuring they’re the ones granting permission to do so.

Access ensures peace of mind. Hubstaff's default settings enable users to view the same information as their managers. The second a timer is stopped, data collection stops, too.

Data collection starts with the end user. Users decide when to clock in and out, take breaks, and submit hours as automated timesheets.
